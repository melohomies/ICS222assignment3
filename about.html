About ekhayachickens Private Limited

ekhayachickens is a poultry productions company that was started in 2018 by Melody Mhasvi in her backyard with a start of 20 birds. The company has now grown and has chicken runs that can house more than 200 birds. It has evolved over the years to the production of day old chicks , table eggs and fertilized eggs. ekhayachickens' main aim is to be one of the leading production companies in the country and hopefully internationally in the upcoming future.
